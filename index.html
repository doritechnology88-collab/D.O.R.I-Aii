Introducing D.O.R.I AI

D.O.R.I AI is an indigenous artificial intelligence developed in India by Dori Technology. Unlike conventional AI platforms, D.O.R.I AI is built without using any third-party AI APIs, ensuring complete independence, transparency, and innovation.

Technology Behind D.O.R.I AI

D.O.R.I AI is powered by DUDR Technology (Dori Ultra Deep Research), a proprietary research framework designed and developed by Dori Technology. DUDR focuses on deep analysis, independent reasoning, and original AI research methodologies.

Launch Status of D.O.R.I AI

The beta version of D.O.R.I AI has already been launched. The beta release does not include full DUDR capabilities. The official launch date of the complete D.O.R.I AI platform with DUDR technology will be announced soon.
